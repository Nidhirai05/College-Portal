BTech - Bachelor of technology

B.Tech. offers various specialisations in the field of technology. Depending on what you are interested in, you can select a B.Tech major. Once you finish this programme, you have the option to seek employment or further your education based on your needs.

The branch available in our college :

  • B.Tech in Civil Engineering
  • B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering
  • B.Tech in Electronics and Communication Engineering
  • B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering
